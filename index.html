Projects

Erth

Demo

A Saudi research platform to document historical sites.

Erth provides a solution for documenting historical sites in Saudi Arabia through a Saudi research platform. The project aims to assess the impact of environmental factors on historical sites in Saudi Arabia. Using an algorithm based on data such as humidity levels, pollution ratios, erosion rates, and carbon isotope ratios, we were able to identify the most vulnerable sites and display them on a map based on documentation priority.
